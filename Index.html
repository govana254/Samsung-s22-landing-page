Premium Materials

The Samsung Galaxy S22 lineup is made of glass front (Gorilla Glass Victus+), glass back (Gorilla Glass Victus) and an aluminium frame. The phone is IP68 dust/water resistant up to 1.5m for 30 mins.

Performance

All three Galaxy S22 models use a new and faster 4nm octa-core processor.This is the Snapdragon 8 Gen 1 in the US and the Exynos 2200 in European markets.These chipsets allows you to enjoy a seamless performance while accessing multiple apps and playing intense graphics games.

Camera

Features on the rear camera setup include Digital Zoom, Auto Flash, Face detection and Touch to focus. On the front, the Samsung phone comes with a 40 MP camera for selfies and video chats.Samsung has doubled down on AI squeezing every ounce of the ISOCELL HM3 sensor's capabilities. The additional tuning makes the main camera more light-sensitive, increasing the amount of light it captures in low-lit environments.
